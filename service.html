Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What types of construction services do you offer?
We specialize in residential, commercial, and civil construction projects including architectural design, structural engineering, interior finishes, electrical and mechanical works, and project management.
How long has Mbandasa Limited been in operation?
Mbandasa Limited has over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, delivering quality and reliable services across Kenya and East Africa.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured to operate as a professional construction company, ensuring compliance with local building regulations and safety standards.
Do you help with architectural drawings and approvals?
Absolutely. We offer architectural drafting services and assist in obtaining the necessary regulatory approvals before construction begins.
How do you ensure the quality of your work?
We follow strict quality control procedures, employ skilled professionals, use high-grade materials, and regularly inspect sites to maintain excellent standards.
Do you offer free project quotations?
Yes, we provide free quotations after an initial site visit or consultation to understand the scope and requirements of your project.
What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary based on the size and complexity of the job. However, we always aim to complete work on schedule while maintaining quality.
Can you work with my budget?
Yes. We provide flexible solutions and accurate estimates to help you achieve your goals within your budget without compromising quality.
Do you handle both new builds and renovations?
Yes, we undertake both new construction projects and renovations for homes, offices, and commercial spaces.
How can I get started with Mbandasa Limited?
Simply contact us through our website, phone, or email to schedule a consultation. We’ll discuss your vision and provide guidance on the next steps.
